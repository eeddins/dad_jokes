An older man at the evening function bowed his head and wept quietly but copiously while while a young woman rendered the plaintive ballad, "My Old Kentucky Home."The hostess tiptoed up to him and inquired tenderly, "Pardon me, are you a Kentuckian?""Nay, madam," the tearful one replied, "I'm a musician."
