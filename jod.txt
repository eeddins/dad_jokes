 After having a pint, he decides to have a little fun.  “I’ll pay 500 dollars to whomever drinks 10 pints of Guinness in 5 minutes”, he says.  Nobody takes him up on his offer but one guy quickly runs out of the pub.  5 minutes later he comes back, says “I’ll do it”, and then proceeds downing 10 pints in 5 minutes.  Impressed, the American pays him the money, and asks “Where did you go right after I made the offer?”  The guy says: “Oh, I just ran to the pub next door to see if I could actually do it.”
