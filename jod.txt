 When grape harvest season arrived, a Bektashi began filling large jars with grape juice. Informed of this, the sultan came to the Bektashi's place and angrily asked:  "Why are you filling these jars with grape juice?"  Caught off guard, the Bektashi nervously replied, "I'm filling them so they'll turn into vinegar."  The sultan, softening a bit, said, "You say vinegar, but what if it turns into wine?"  Seeing the sultan's softened demeanor, the Bektashi smiled and said, "Well, that's up to God!"
